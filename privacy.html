Your Privacy Matters

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 3, 2026

Wanderlust Travel ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using our services:

  • Account Registration: Name, email address, and password when you create an account via our signup form or Google Sign-In
  • Booking Requests: Full name, email address, check-in/check-out dates, number of travelers, trip type, and special requests when you submit a booking request
  • Contact Form: First name, last name, email address, subject, and message when you contact us
  • Newsletter: Email address when you subscribe to our newsletter
  • Travel Journal: Journal titles, destinations, trip types, dates, and personal travel notes
  • Packing Lists: Custom packing list items and categories you create
  • Budget Tracker: Budget amounts, expense categories, and financial notes you enter

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

We automatically collect and store the following information in your browser:

  • Search History: Your search queries and search timestamps
  • Recently Viewed Destinations: Names, images, and types of destinations you view
  • Favorites: Destinations you mark as favorites
  • Theme Preference: Your light or dark mode selection
  • Authentication Time: Timestamp of when you last logged in

1.3 Information from Third Parties

If you choose to sign in using Google, we may receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google's authentication service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Wanderlust Travel website
  • Personalize your experience with destination recommendations, favorites, and recently viewed items
  • Process and store your booking requests, journal entries, packing lists, and budget plans
  • Send newsletter content and travel inspiration (if you subscribe)
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Remember your theme preference for consistent appearance

3. How We Store Your Data

3.1 Client-Side Storage (LocalStorage)

All data collected by Wanderlust Travel is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device — it is not transmitted to any server
  • Only you can access your data through your browser
  • Data persists across browser sessions on the same device
  • Data is not shared with any third party
  • Clearing your browser data will permanently delete all stored information

3.2 LocalStorage Keys Used

The following localStorage keys are used by our application:

  • wanderlust_user — Your account session (name, email, provider)
  • wanderlust_auth_time — Login timestamp
  • wanderlust_remember — Remembered email for login
  • wanderlust_favorites — Array of favorited destination names
  • wanderlust_bookings — Array of booking request records
  • wanderlust_subscribers — Array of newsletter subscriber emails
  • wanderlust_searchHistory — Array of recent search queries
  • wanderlust_recentlyViewed — Array of recently viewed destinations
  • wanderlust_journal — Array of travel journal entries
  • wanderlust_packingLists — Packing list data
  • wanderlust_budget — Budget tracking data
  • wanderlust_darkMode — Theme preference setting
  • wanderlust_contactMessages — Contact form submissions

3.3 No Server-Side Storage

We do not operate any backend servers or databases. All processing and storage happens locally in your browser. No personal data is transmitted to external servers for storage or processing.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

4.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third parties.

4.2 Third-Party Services

Our website loads resources from the following third-party services, which may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Fonts (fonts.google.com) — Loads font files. May log your IP address and browser information. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Unsplash (images.unsplash.com) — Serves destination images. May collect usage analytics. See Unsplash Privacy Policy.
  • OpenStreetMap / Leaflet (openstreetmap.org) — Provides map tiles. May log your IP address for tile requests. See OSMF Privacy Policy.
  • Google Maps (maps.google.com) — External map links open in a new tab. Subject to Google Privacy Policy.

4.3 Social Media Links

Links to our social media profiles (Instagram, Facebook, YouTube) open in new tabs. These platforms have their own privacy policies and data collection practices.

4.4 Legal Disclosure

Since all data is stored locally in your browser, we do not have access to your personal data and cannot disclose it. However, data stored in your browser may be accessible to others who have access to your device or browser.

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view all your stored data through the various sections of the Site (Favorites, My Bookings, Journal, Packing, Budget, Account)
  • Delete: You can delete individual items (e.g., remove favorites, delete journal entries, clear bookings) through the respective sections
  • Clear All Data: You can clear all stored data by clearing your browser's localStorage or site data through your browser settings
  • Modify: You can update your account information and edit journal entries
  • Opt Out: You can unsubscribe from the newsletter by not providing your email
  • Do Not Use: You can choose not to create an account or use any features that collect personal data

6. Children's Privacy

Wanderlust Travel is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information from the relevant browser storage.

7. Data Security

7.1 Storage Limitations

Because all data is stored in your browser's localStorage, it is not protected by server-side security measures. Anyone with access to your device and browser can potentially view this data.

7.2 Your Responsibility

You are responsible for:

  • Securing your device and browser from unauthorized access
  • Using strong, unique passwords for your account
  • Logging out when using shared or public devices
  • Clearing browser data when using public or shared computers

7.3 XSS Protection

We implement HTML escaping (XSS protection) in our code to prevent malicious scripts from being injected into stored data and displayed on the page.

8. Cookies & Local Storage

8.1 No Tracking Cookies

Wanderlust Travel does not use cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising purposes.

8.2 LocalStorage Usage

We use browser localStorage exclusively for storing your preferences, account session, and user-generated content. This is essential for the Site's functionality and cannot be disabled without breaking core features.

8.3 Third-Party Cookies

Third-party services loaded by our Site (Google Fonts, Unsplash, OpenStreetMap) may set their own cookies. We do not control these cookies and recommend reviewing the respective privacy policies of these services.

9. Web Share & Clipboard

When you use the "Share" feature for a destination:

  • If your browser supports the Web Share API, the share dialog is handled by your operating system
  • If Web Share is not available, the destination link is copied to your clipboard
  • No share data is transmitted to our servers

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
